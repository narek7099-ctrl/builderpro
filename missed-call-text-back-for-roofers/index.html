Frequently asked questions

What is missed call text-back?

It's an automation that instantly sends a text message to anyone who calls your roofing business when you can't answer, keeping the lead engaged instead of letting them call a competitor.

Why does this matter for roofers?

Roofing leads are time-sensitive and most callers won't leave a voicemail. A reply in seconds dramatically improves the odds you keep the job.
